A read on where Encore's methodology could travel further than Jay's calendar.

Encore AI Labs transforms AI-underserved industries. That is the line on the site, repeated a few times, and it is true. The harder problem is the one under it: the methodology is in one head, pipeline is founder-gated, and governance posture is implicit rather than published. A two-person shop with four verticals can only compress so far without something to license, hand off, or co-deliver against.

The frame

Most AI work lives on three layers.

Encore already knows this because the work in legal and CPG keeps landing on the first two layers. The leverage is not more capacity. The leverage is a written methodology that another analyst, another consultant, or a partner firm can execute against without Jay on the call.

Case

Feeld's CTO bought the scope once and kept using it.

We ran a scoped sprint with Andrew Santus, CTO at Feeld. Workshop, advisory calls, an Organizational Context Architecture, and a Strategic Operations Framework. The deliverable was not a build. It was a document the CTO kept and now uses to scope every internal AI decision.
